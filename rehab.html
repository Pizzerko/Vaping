If You Are Addicted...How Do You Quit?

1. Find A Supportive Group

Friends and family are more than happy to help you quit vape and live healthier. School may offer comfortable and safe spaces for you to comfortable start your vape free journey.

2. Find New Habits

Setting a date to quit vaping is an important first step. First, give yourself time to get ready to quit vaping. This ensures you have the confidence and support needed to fulfill this successfully. Choose a date that is isn't far away, but close enough where you have time to feel ready - preferably no more than a week or two away. Avoid picking a quit date that will be stressful and choose a date where you can fully focus on ignoring the cravings.

3. Seek Professional Help

Don't feel discouraged to take on this journey yourself. There are many willing to help you live healthy and stay drug-free. Visit a doctor or talk with a tobacco cessation counselor. Numbers like 1-800-QUIT-NOW or websites like theTruth.com are open 24/7 for support.
